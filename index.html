HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO REPAIR MY COMPUTER ?

Most repairs take 1-2 days to complete. There are situations where the repairs may take longer such as when parts have to be ordered. we strives to only use high quality, often original equipment replacement parts and not use cheap, knock-off, low quality parts that will only fail in a short amount of time.
